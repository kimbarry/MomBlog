Welcome to my blog

Parenting during my time on Voyager in the Delta Quadrant has been a unique and challenging experience. As a Starfleet officer and a single mother to my daughter, Naomi, I navigate the complexities of raising her in an unpredictable and often dangerous environment. The isolation of the Delta Quadrant has fostered a close bond between us; we rely on each other for emotional support amidst the constant threat of alien encounters and the uncertainty of our journey home. I strive to provide a sense of normalcy and stability for Naomi, introducing her to the wonders of space exploration while instilling in her the values of resilience, courage, and compassion. The crew of Voyager has become our extended family, offering a supportive community that helps us share in both the joys and challenges of life among the stars.
